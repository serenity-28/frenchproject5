Greetings/Formalities

  • Hello: Nǐ hǎo
  • Good morning: Zǎoshang hǎo
    • Goodbye: Zàijiàn Thank you: Xièxiè Good evening: Wǎnshàng hǎo Please: Qǐng Excuse me: Duìbùqǐ (can also mean "sorry" in some contexts) / Láojià (when asking for someone's attention) Ma’am: Nǚshì Sir: Xiānshēng Yes: Shì / Duì No: Bù Sorry: Duìbùqǐ

Places

  • Restaurant: Restaurant: Cāntīng
  • Hotel: Jiǔdiàn
  • Airport: Jīchǎng
  • Shop: Shāngdiàn
  • Park: Gōngyuán
  • Train station: Huǒchēzhàn

Helpful Phrases

  • Can you help me?: Can you help me?: Nǐ néng bāng wǒ ma?
  • Do you speak English?: Nǐ huì shuō Yīngyǔ ma?
  • Can you repeat, please?: Nǐ kěyǐ chóngfù yīxià ma?
  • Where are the toilets?: Cèsuǒ zài nǎlǐ?
  • Where is…?: … zài nǎlǐ?
  • How much does it cost?: Duōshǎo qián?
  • Sorry, I do not understand: Duìbùqǐ, wǒ tīng bù dǒng
  • A ticket, please: Qǐng gěi wǒ yī zhāng piào
  • Can you show me the way?: Nǐ néng zhǐdiǎn wǒ zěnme zǒu ma?
  • Sorry, I do not speak: Duìbùqǐ, wǒ bù huì shuō…

Directions

  • Here: Zhèlǐ
  • There: Nàlǐ
  • On the right: Zài yòubiān
  • On the left: Zài zuǒbiān
  • Straight ahead: Yìzhí zǒu
  • At the corner: Zài lùkǒu
  • One, two, three, four blocks away: Lí zhèlǐ yī, èr, sān, sì tiáo jiē
